Safety Tips for Renting in Bali

  • Always check the scooter/car condition before renting
  • Take photos of any existing damage
  • Ensure you have valid insurance coverage
  • Carry your international driving license
  • Wear a helmet at all times (required by law)
  • Be cautious on rainy days - roads can be slippery
